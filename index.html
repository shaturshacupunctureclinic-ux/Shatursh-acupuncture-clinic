Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first visit to Shatursh Healthcare Centre.

Is acupuncture painful?
Most patients feel little to no pain during acupuncture. The needles used are ultra-fine — much thinner than hypodermic needles. You may feel a brief pinch, tingling, or a warm sensation at the needle site, which is actually a sign the therapy is working. Our practitioners are highly experienced in minimizing any discomfort.
How many sessions do I need to see results?
Many patients experience significant relief within 3–5 sessions. However, the number of sessions varies based on your condition — acute issues may respond in 1–3 sessions while chronic conditions may require 8–12. After your initial consultation, we'll give you a realistic treatment timeline tailored to your specific situation.
Are all treatments completely drug-free and safe?
Yes, absolutely. All our therapies are 100% natural, non-invasive, and drug-free. We use only sterile, single-use, disposable needles for acupuncture (so there's zero infection risk). Our treatments are suitable for adults, children, elderly patients, and even pregnant women (with certain modifications). Side effects are extremely rare and minimal.
What conditions do you treat?
We treat a wide range of conditions including chronic pain, migraine, sciatica, frozen shoulder, arthritis, neck/back pain, joint pain, sports injuries, stress, anxiety, poor posture, nerve pain, fatigue, and much more. Our seven therapy modalities give us the ability to address almost any musculoskeletal or neurological condition naturally. Use our Symptom Finder above to discover what's right for you.
How long does a treatment session last?
A typical session lasts between 45 to 60 minutes. Your very first visit includes a thorough consultation and assessment which may take 60–90 minutes. Follow-up sessions are generally shorter. We ensure you never feel rushed — your healing deserves full attention.
Do I need to prepare anything before my first visit?
We recommend wearing loose, comfortable clothing so our practitioners can easily access treatment areas. Have a light meal 1–2 hours before your session (don't come on a completely empty stomach). Bring any medical reports or prescriptions you have. Avoid alcohol or heavy exercise on the day of treatment. Most importantly — come with an open mind and relax!
Can I combine multiple therapies in one visit?
Yes! In fact, combining therapies often produces the best results. For example, cupping followed by acupuncture is highly effective for pain and sports injuries. Our specialist will design the optimal combination for your condition during your consultation. Multi-therapy sessions are available and highly recommended for complex or chronic conditions.

(3) Call us directly at +91 98765 43210. Walk-ins are also welcome, but we recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot.
